ML is a subset of AI where system learns from patterns of data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.

ML = Learning from Data -> Predict Outcomes

Examples: 
-> Email Spam Detection
-> Content Recommendation
-> Weather Report Prediction
-> Cricket Score Prediction

ML is a part or subset of AI

AI is broader field of creating machines that can think, reason and act like humans.

Examples:
-> Voice Assistants (Siri, OK Google, Alexa)
-> Chatbots (LLMs)
-> Self-driving Cars (Tesla)

Types of Machine Learning:
1) Supervised Learning
2) Unsupervised Learning
3) Reinforcement Learning

1) SL: Models learns from labeled data (input + correct output)

Types of SL:
Regression & Classification

Examples:
1) Email Spam Detection
2) House Price / Stock Price Predictions

2) Unsupervised Learning: Model finds patterns from unlabelled data

Types:
Clustering & Association

Examples:
1) Product Recommendation
2) Customer Segmentation
3) Music Playlist Recommendation

3) Reinforcement Learning: Model learns using trial and error method with rewards and penalties

Examples:
1) AI Game Opponent (Chess, Carrom, Battle Royale Games)
2) Self-driving Cars
3) Robotics
4) Stock Trading Bots
